About Hack4Impact India

Hack4Impact India is a 24-hour national level hackathon that brings together innovative minds from across the country to solve real-world challenges through technology and creativity.

The hackathon features four focused tracks — Defence, Agriculture, Sustainability, and Healthcare — encouraging participants to develop impactful solutions in critical sectors that shape the future.

Open to all students, Hack4Impact India provides a collaborative environment to ideate, build, and present meaningful projects. Join us to innovate, compete, and create solutions that make a real difference.

Open to All Branches

Students from any college, any branch, and any year can participate (but only hardware)

Hackathon Tracks

Develop hardware and embedded solutions using industry-standard EDA tools and ECE technologies

Health Sector

Design low-power bio-medical sensors and hardware systems for patient monitoring using circuit design and simulation tools.

Defence

Build secure communication hardware, radar signal processing units, and rugged embedded systems.

Sustainability

Create energy-efficient circuits and power management systems for green electronics and smart grids.

Agriculture

Develop IoT-based soil monitoring sensors and automated hardware systems for smart farming.
